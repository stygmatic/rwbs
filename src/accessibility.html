Accessibility

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Space Pickles Rocketry is committed to making our website accessible to everyone. We strive to ensure that our content is available and usable for all visitors, regardless of their abilities or the technology they use.

Accessibility Features

  • High contrast text and background colors
  • Clear and consistent navigation
  • Alt text for all images
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Responsive design for all devices

Browser Compatibility

Our website is designed to work with all modern web browsers, including:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any accessibility issues while using our website, please let us know:

  • Email us at accessibility@spacepickles.org
  • Use our contact form and select "Accessibility Issue" as the subject
  • Include specific details about the issue and the page where you encountered it

Standards Compliance

Our website is designed to meet or exceed the following accessibility standards:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • ADA Title III requirements

Need Help?

If you need assistance accessing any part of our website, please don't hesitate to contact us.
