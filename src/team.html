Our Team

Meet Space Pickles Rocketry

We are a diverse team of high school students passionate about rocketry and aerospace engineering. Our members come from various backgrounds and bring unique skills to the table.

Our Subteams

Electrical Division

Development of electronic systems, sensors, and control mechanisms for our rockets

Design Division

Design and fabrication of rocket airframes, structural components, and aerodynamic elements

Software Division

Development of flight software, data analysis tools, and simulation systems

Team Leadership

Team Captain

Overall team coordination and leadership

Chief Engineer

Technical oversight and engineering decisions

Operations Manager

Launch operations and safety coordination
