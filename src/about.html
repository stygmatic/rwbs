About Space Pickles Rocketry

Our Mission

Space Pickles Rocketry is a student organization at American High School dedicated to designing, building, and launching high-powered rockets. Our team competes in national and international competitions, including the Spaceport America Cup, and works on innovative rocket designs and propulsion systems.

Our History

Space Pickles Rocketry was founded by a group of passionate aerospace engineering students at American High School who wanted to apply their classroom knowledge to real-world rocket design and construction. Since our founding, we have grown into one of the leading high school rocketry teams in California.

Our Goals

  • Design and launch increasingly sophisticated rockets
  • Compete successfully in national and international rocketry competitions
  • Provide hands-on engineering experience to American High School students
  • Develop innovative solutions to rocketry challenges
  • Foster a collaborative environment for learning and growth
